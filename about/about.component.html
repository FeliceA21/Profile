About

People say I could last a day without talking. This is partially true. From childhood, I am always alone and

I loved to explore things that kids of the same age doesn’t like. I like to know how things work; how it is

made and how it is used. I am a person who likes to dream of things that the world can offer. I am an

ambitious person. I always say to myself, “dream big and make it happen”.

I am serious and disciplined. Loyalty is probably my biggest virtue. I give it to the fullest and also expect

the same from the people I give it to. I show utmost care and love to the people that matter to me. I will

do anything in my power to keep them happy.

I am not perfect though. I can be moody when I don’t achieve whatever I want to accomplish in life. My

biggest weakness is that I find it difficult to trust people. We are always surrounded by dishonest

people; you can never know who they are and what they are planning for you. I’d rather come face to

face with a killer, than be stabbed in the back by a pretender.
